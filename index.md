- Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bologna, studying the role of firms' beliefs in  low-carbon transition as part of the [BELIEFS project](https://site.unibo.it/beliefs/).
- Previously, I was a research fellow at the University of Verona under the project “Conditional cooperation, social norms, and sustainable behavior”.
- I completed my PhD at the University of East Anglia, under the supervision of [Theodore Turocy](https://research-portal.uea.ac.uk/en/persons/theodore-turocy/), [Robert Sugden](https://research-portal.uea.ac.uk/en/persons/robert-sugden/), and [Amrish Patel](https://research-portal.uea.ac.uk/en/persons/amrish-patel/).
- You can find my [CV here](assets/CV.pdf){:download}.

<div style="clear: both;"></div>

My research focuses on behaviour and expectations in the context of cooperation and sustainability. Using experimental methods I investigate how beliefs, norms, and institutions affect cooperation and climate-related behaviour.

You can contact me at [andrea.mariettaleina@unibo.it](mailto:andrea.mariettaleina@unibo.it).
