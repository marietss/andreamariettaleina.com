- Postodoctoral researcher at the University of Bologna, studying the role of beliefs in climate transition under the [BELIEFS project](https://site.unibo.it/beliefs/).
- PhD at the University of East Anglia, under the supervision of [Theodore Turocy](https://research-portal.uea.ac.uk/en/persons/theodore-turocy/), [Robert Sugden](https://research-portal.uea.ac.uk/en/persons/robert-sugden/), and [Amrish Patel](https://research-portal.uea.ac.uk/en/persons/amrish-patel/).
- Previously, Research fellow at the University of Verona under the project “Conditional cooperation, social norms, and sustainable behavior”.
- You can find my [CV here](assets/CV.pdf){:download}.

I am interested in behaviours and expectations in the context of cooperation and sustainability. I use experimental methods to investigate how beliefs, social norms, and institutions affect cooperation and climate in social dilemmas.
